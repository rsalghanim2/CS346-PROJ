TThe Abha cable car is one of the interesting experiences that tourism in Abha offers to all its visitors. The experience of riding this cable car will give you wonderful views from a height of 1600 The ride lasts for 25 to 30 minutes, and once you reach the top of the hill, you will find a number of restaurants and food stalls that offer everything from snacks, drinks, and ice cream. .


Andalus Park

Andalus Park is characterized by wide green spaces, a calm atmosphere, in addition to its distinctive location and containing a large number of restaurants, not only this, but also includes areas designated for exercise, walking paths, and a games area far from the public street to suit children to play safely .
