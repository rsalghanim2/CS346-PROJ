About Jeddah


The all-year-round warm city of Jeddah, the captivating hub, invites you to be part of the millions of worldwide visitors from traders and explorers since ancient times. Jeddah is the birthplace of worldwide arts and music, and a gathering spot for multi-vibrant cultures, a unique blend that left its mark on Jeddah’s exquisite cuisine of many fine dishes with global tastes. Here, you will have a chance to see the best view out there of the colorful sunset shades shimmering off the divine Red Sea shores.

Restaurants & Cafes

Kuuru

Japanese and Peruvian cuisines meld to form an authentic new culinary experience known as Nikkei cooking. Theatrical, colorful, and dramatically delicious, this is fresh, modern luxury dining, inspired by tradition and waiting to be discovered.

Kabana

Afghan fusion cuisine with a Mediterranean twist. One of the most massive upscale Fusion food destinations in all of Jeddah, it offers a distinctive experience with dishes made for sharing in its comfortable and elegant atmosphere.

Nobu

Nobu branch, the world’s most recognized Japanese restaurant, known for its innovative new-style cuisine. Located on the popular Jeddah Corniche the heart of Al Nawras Resort, the most iconic resort in Jeddah overlooking the Jeddah Corniche.

Nawa Patio

Inspired by ancient civilizations such as the Sumerians, Thamud, Lihyan, and Nabataeans. And the use of plastic is avoided wherever possible. Diners are also encouraged to take home their excess food or give it to local food banks, charity organizations, and humanitarian programs.

Chant Bistro

An exciting experience for breakfast, lunch, bakeries and sweets. Chant Bistro Restaurant is located in the city of Jeddah, Al-Naim District, Al-Amal Road. The restaurant offers distinctive types and a varied menu that serves breakfast.

Broots Café

Unique in serving chocolate-based beverages and products, and its freshly baked pastries have very high and positive reviews. These include: vegan banana cakes and gluten-free chocolate cakes. The café has that African feel which is reflected on its wall decorations.

C.Hub

The reward of your day; its menu is full of the best carefully selected breakfast options or low-fat meals, while maintaining excellent coffee that will always be among your favorites.

Medd Café & Roastery

one of Jeddah’s most popular coffee shops. Its laid-back setup and friendly atmosphere is a great way to start or end a long day at work. The concept of the café is to maintain a minimalistic vibe with absolutely no distractions from its exceptional coffee.

Cup & Couch Cafe

the go-to neighborhood café, creating a comfortable space complete with mountains of pillows, stacks of board games, and really comfortable couches! What’s special about Cup & Couch is the fact that it is family-run.



Hotels

Rosewood Hotel

Where the desert meets the Red Sea, all the amenities and attractions a leisure or business traveler desires are close at hand in Jeddah. You are invited to explore this historic city filled with cultural and artistic sites.

InterContinental

Enjoy breathtaking views of the Red Sea front and King Fahad's Fountain. Nestled in a prime location close to Jeddah business centers and top attractions such as Al Balad, shopping malls and famous historical sites, with fast access to King Abdulaziz International Airport.

Sheraton

With an exclusive location on the fashionable North Corniche and breathtaking views of the Red Sea, Sheraton Jeddah Hotel boasts 172 rooms and suites featuring Nespresso machines, comfortable work spaces and memorable views of the city and corniche.

Waldorf Astoria

Arrive to elegance under a three-story Swarovski chandelier and wake up to views of Jeddah. Adorned with gold accents, Italian furnishings and glistening crystal, our resort is an oasis on the Red Sea.Enjoy personalized butler service and modern amenities including an EV Charging station.

Park Hyatt

Experience the art of personalized luxury at the award-winning Park Hyatt Jeddah-Marina, Club & Spa. This multi-faceted 5-star resort is situated in beautiful and tranquil landscaped gardens on the sea shore of Jeddah’s Corniche, views of the Red Sea and King Fahad fountain.

Movenpick Resort

Welcome to your dream island. The private island resort combines Arabian tradition with Swiss luxury and hospitality, so you can enjoy unforgettable moments in paradise.
